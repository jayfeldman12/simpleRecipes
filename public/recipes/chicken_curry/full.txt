Chicken Curry Recipe Full Text

Ingredients:
- Chicken: 500g
- Curry Powder: 2 tbsp
- Coconut milk: 400ml
- Onion: 1 large
- Garlic: 3 cloves
- Ginger: 1 inch piece

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a pot and saut√© onions until soft.
2. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 2 minutes.
3. Add chicken and curry powder; cook until the chicken is browned.
4. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 25 minutes.
5. Serve with rice.

Enjoy your Chicken Curry!
