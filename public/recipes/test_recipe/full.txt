Ingredients:
- Flour: 500g
- Sugar: 100g
- Salt: 1 tsp
- Butter: 200g
- Eggs: 4
- Milk: 300ml
- Baking Powder: 2 tsp
- Vanilla Extract: 1 tsp
- Cocoa Powder: 100g
- Olive Oil: 50ml
- Water: 250ml
- Yeast: 7g
- Honey: 3 tbsp
- Lemon Zest: 1 tsp
- Nuts: 100g
- Cinnamon: 1/2 tsp
- Nutmeg: 1/4 tsp
- Rice Flour: 150g
- Cornstarch: 50g
- Soy Sauce: 2 tbsp

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 180Â°C.
2. Sift the flour and baking powder into a bowl.
3. Mix in the sugar and salt.
4. Add the butter and mix until crumbly.
5. Beat the eggs in a separate bowl.
6. Mix in the milk gradually.
7. Stir in vanilla extract and lemon zest.
8. Combine the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients.
9. Add the cocoa powder and mix thoroughly.
10. Drizzle in olive oil and water slowly.
11. Knead the dough until smooth.
12. Let the dough rest for 15 minutes.
13. Sprinkle yeast over the dough and fold lightly.
14. Add honey and mix well.
15. Fold in the nuts carefully.
16. Sprinkle cinnamon and nutmeg evenly.
17. Mix in rice flour and cornstarch.
18. Add soy sauce for a unique flavor.
19. Shape the dough into small portions.
20. Place portions on a baking tray and bake for 25 minutes.
