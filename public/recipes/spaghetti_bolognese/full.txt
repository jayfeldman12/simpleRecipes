Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe Full Text

Ingredients:
- Spaghetti: 200g
- Ground beef: 300g
- Tomato sauce: 400ml
- Onion: 1 medium
- Garlic: 2 cloves

Instructions:
1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Add onions, garlic, and beef; cook until browned.
3. Stir in tomato sauce; simmer for 20 minutes.
4. Serve sauce over cooked spaghetti.

Enjoy your meal!
