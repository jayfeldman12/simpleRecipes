Pancakes Recipe Full Text

Ingredients:
- Flour: 1 cup
- Egg: 1 large
- Milk: 1 cup
- Butter: 2 tbsp
- Sugar: 1 tbsp
- Salt: a pinch

Instructions:
1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
2. Beat in the egg and milk until smooth.
3. Melt butter and stir it into the mixture.
4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle over medium-high heat.
5. Pour batter onto the griddle and cook until brown on both sides.

Enjoy your pancakes!
